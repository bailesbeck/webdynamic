How to Replace RAM

Step 1

Power off your computer and unplug it.

Step 2

Open the case or back panel with a screwdriver.

Step 3

Locate the RAM slots.

Step 4

Remove old RAM by clicking end holders back and insert new sticks, making sure to flip end holders back up.

Step 5

Close the case and power on.
